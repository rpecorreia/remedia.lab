Objetives

The Remedia.Lab main objectives are the following:






To contribute to the diagnosis of the current situation of regional media.


To promote experimental tools and innovative strategies to strengthen the local and regional media business model, improving its sustainability.


To increase their innovation degree and to improve their connection with new publics, considering the social and cultural changes of the Portuguese Centre Region.





To increase the innovation present in local and regional media, using information and communication technologies.


To incubate new projects of regional media with an innovative profile, using information and communication technologies.






Outcomes

Among the outcomes of the project, we highlight the following:

Regional Inquiry

A regional inquiry to find the working conditions of the professionals working in local and regional media from the Centre of Portugal.

Focus Groups

Focus groups with journalists, divided by the several administrative units.

Analysis

The analysis of the information available in the regional press portal, regarding the Centre Region.

New Strategies and Devices

Developing new strategies and devices, including:
a) Strategies of multimedia news reporting;
b) Strategies to assure interactivity with publics;
c) Specific digital contents directed to well-defined new publics with new agendas;
d) Online business models and self-promoting strategies to attract new publics.

Pratical Course Unity

Implementation of an experimental and practical course unity directed to communication students and journalists.

National Encounter

First National Meeting of Journalists of Regional Media held.
