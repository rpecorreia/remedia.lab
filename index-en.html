An experienced American journalist in the midst of about two dozen regional media journalists from the center, to reflect on “The elements of journalism in the new digital ecosystem”. This was the training cycle led by Walter Dean, which ran from September 6 to December 20, 2019.


The importance of journalism and the way journalists do their work were at the heart of the initiative, promoted by Re/media.Lab. Very much based on practical cases and the debate among all the participants, this cycle was well received and participated. In the end, the balance was positive and the possibility of repeating the training in the future remained open.




The importance of local journalism

The characteristics that a journalist must have, the differences between exercise journalism in the USA and Europe and the importance of local journalism, were some of the topics addressed by Walter Dean, in an interview he gave to the participants, at the end of the last session. We then share the result of about 30 minutes of conversation.
